# BDM Compass: Operating Manual & Strategic Protocol
**Standardizing Elite Performance for Sales Professionals**

---

## 1. The Philosophy: Sales Professional vs. Sales Person
BDM Compass is not an activity tracker; it is a **Governance Node** designed to transition your team from "Sales People" to **"Sales Professionals."**

*   **Sales People** focus on volume, "busy work," and lagging indicators like trailing revenue.
*   **Sales Professionals** focus on **Strategic Yield**, **Stakeholder Mapping**, and **Velocity**. They protect their time as their most valuable asset and provide "Elite Forecast Reliability" to the territory.

---

## 2. The Weekly Success Loop (Required Cadence)

To maintain territory visibility, all users must adhere to the following rhythm:

### Monday: Preparation & Snapshot
*   **Action**: Review the **Intelligence Hub**. The AI synthesizes the previous week’s trends and deal risk vectors.
*   **Action**: Set **Weekly Goals** in the dashboard. These should focus on "Leading Indicators" (e.g., "Map 3 stakeholders at Rio Tinto").
*   **Output**: Generate the **Weekly Pack** (PDF) to ground the week in data.

### Tuesday – Thursday: High-Velocity Execution
*   **Action**: Log activities (Calls, Site Visits) in the **Rapid Habit Tracker**.
*   **Action**: Perform **SPIN Call Planning** for all major account meetings. 
*   **Protocol**: Every call plan must be printed and taken into the field to ensure solution-based conversation standards.

### Friday: The Strategy Synthesis (The Friday Review)
*   **Action**: In the **Pipeline Ledger**, select the **Top 8** opportunities for the Friday Review meeting.
*   **Action**: Perform **Rollover Logic** for active deals still in progress.
*   **Action**: Archive any "Closed Lost" deals with a clear strategic reason for the win-back ledger.

## 3. High-Density Feature Guide

### Call Planning Center (SPIN Model)
Elite preparation is the hallmark of a professional. Use the SPIN model to move conversations from "Price" to "Implication":
1.  **Situation**: Establish the customer's current state.
2.  **Problem**: Identify the specific gaps.
3.  **Implication**: Quantify the **Cost of Inaction**.
4.  **Need-Payoff**: Reveal the value of the solution.

### Pipeline Strategy Ledger
Standardized to the **Millions (M)** scale for executive visibility:
*   **Salesforce Routing**: Clicking an account name performs an **Intelligent ID Search**. If an Opportunity ID is detected, it opens the record directly.
*   **Drill-down Ledger**: Click any KPI total to see the calculation ledger—a transparent audit of every deal contributing to that result.

## 4. Governance & Data Integrity (Admin Only)

### Master Territory Sync (CRM Importer)
*   **Protocol**: Use the **Overwrite Strategy**. The system identifies accounts using the `account_master_code`. New uploads update existing records instantly.
*   **Ghost Revenue**: Accounts not mapped to a BDM are automatically routed to the **"Not Managed"** user node. This node must be reviewed weekly for unassigned potential.

### Duplicate Manager
*   **Audit Logic**: The system identifies redundancies based on **Account Master Code** collisions across the territory.
*   **Action**: Perform a weekly "Smart Purge" to preserve the newest record and maintain territory data integrity.

### User Provisioning
*   New team members must be provisioned with an **Annual Strategic Target**. This drives the achievement metrics and achievement-per-activity calculations.

## 5. Strategic KPI Glossary

| Metric | Calculation | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strategic Yield** | (Portfolio Value) / (Activity) | Measures impact vs. busyness. |
| **Stakeholder Coverage** | % of deals with 3+ Roles | Prevents "Single-Threaded" deal risk. |
| **Discovery Velocity** | Days in Stage | Identifies deals that need to "Fail Fast." |
| **Achievement** | (YTD Actual) / (Annual Tgt) | Current pace against strategic objectives in Millions (M). |
